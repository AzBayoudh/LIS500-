Teachable Machine: Voice Classifier

On this page, we are using Google’s Teachable Machine to classify microphone input into three classes: YES, NO, and QUIET. The emoji in the box below updates based on what the model “hears.”

Live Demo

When your browser asks for microphone permission, click Allow. Then try saying “YES,” “NO,” or staying quiet and watch how the emoji changes.

The canvas below is generated with p5.js and ml5.js and shows our live Teachable Machine sound classifier.
