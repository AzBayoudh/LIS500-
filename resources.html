This page contains resources on Implicit Bias and Intersectionality.

What is Implicit Bias?

  • Implicit bias affects the ways we think, act, and make decisions, often without us even realizing it.
  • Understanding this concept is a very important step in creating more inclusive communities.

    • Implicity Bias: American Psychological Association

    What is Intersectionality?

  • Intersectionality is a framework that shows how different aspects of a person's identity such as race, gender, class, and sexuality interact and shape their experiences.
  • This concept helps us to understand how different parts of a person's identity work together to shape their experiences and the challenges they may face in life.

    • What is Intersectionality Explained | Definition and Examples

    Implicit Bias Test (IBT)

  • An Implicit Bias Test (IBT) is an online quiz that helps to measure the automatic associations in your mind, which reveal hidden biases you might not know you have.
  • This test can help people better understand their own unconscious attitudes and biases, which is a great first step towards leading a less biased life.
